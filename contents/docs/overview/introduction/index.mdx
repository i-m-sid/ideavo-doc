# Introduction

Ideavo is an AI agent that can do idea validation and convert ideas to production-ready applications.

## What is Ideavo?

Ideavo operates in two modes:

**Build Mode**: Converts your idea into a fully functional application using AI agents that generate production-ready code.

**Validate Mode**: Analyzes your idea with comprehensive market research and scoring across 7 business factors.

![Ideavo Platform Overview](/images/ideavo-overview.png)

The platform combines AI-powered development with intelligent idea validation, allowing you to both validate market potential and build applications in a single workflow.
